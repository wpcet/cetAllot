Project Context

This document outlines finalization, deployment, and ongoing technical support for the Working Professionals (WP) Admission Portal at the College of Engineering Trivandrum (CET).

The portal's initial code base was developed by three graduated MCA students. The system has been integrated, updated with the newly added M.Tech admission module, and prepared for final deployment by Muhammed Ajmal U K. The portal is managed by the MCA Department at CET. Technical support and operational maintenance are committed for a duration of 10 months.
